Learn how to create amazing posters with Mini Visionary AI.

Getting Started

1 Describe Your Vision

Type a detailed description of the poster you want to create. Be specific about style, colors, mood, and theme.

2 Add Reference Images (Optional)

Click the + button below the input to upload reference images that can guide the style of your poster.

3 Generate

Click the Generate button and wait for the AI to create your poster. This usually takes 10-30 seconds.

4 Save & Share

Save your favorite posters to your library and download them for use anywhere.

Tips for Better Results

Be Descriptive: Include details about colors, mood, style (vintage, modern, minimalist, etc.)

Use References: Upload images that match the aesthetic you're going for.

Experiment: Try different prompts and styles to see what works best.

Use Quick Actions: Click the pre-made chips like "Movie Poster" or "Horror Theme" for inspiration.

Credits System

Each poster generation costs 10 credits. You can view your remaining credits in the header and purchase more in the Store.
